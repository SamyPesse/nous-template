# Health

Use this folder for health-related context that helps with family assistance while respecting privacy.

## What To Put Here

- High-level care preferences and constraints.
- Allergies, dietary restrictions, accessibility needs, and emergency-relevant notes.
- Doctor, dentist, pharmacy, insurance, or appointment references when appropriate.
- Links or summaries pointing to authoritative medical records stored elsewhere.

## What To Read From Here

Read this folder before meal planning, travel planning, childcare planning, emergency preparation, or anything that could affect wellbeing.

## Privacy Boundary

Avoid copying full medical records into this repository. Do not store government IDs, insurance card images, patient portal passwords, or record numbers unless the user explicitly chooses this repository for that purpose.
