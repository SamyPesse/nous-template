# Profiles

Use this folder for one primary profile per person the assistant may need to understand or help.

## What To Put Here

- Stable identity and context: preferred name, family role, languages, location, broad availability, and durable personal details.
- Pointers to related files in `preferences/`, `routines/`, `health/`, `education/`, `work/`, and `contacts/`.
- Important caveats an assistant should know before making plans or suggestions.

## What To Read From Here

Read relevant profiles before giving personal recommendations, scheduling family logistics, drafting messages, or interpreting household context. Profiles are the first stop for who someone is; specialized folders are the source for domain-specific facts.

## What Not To Put Here

Do not turn profile files into diaries, medical histories, document archives, or task lists. Put shared or time-specific information in the appropriate folder and link to it.
