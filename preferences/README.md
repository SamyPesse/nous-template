# Preferences

Use this folder for personal and family preferences that help the assistant make better recommendations.

## What To Put Here

- Food preferences, allergies cross-links, clothing sizes, gift preferences, entertainment tastes, and travel preferences.
- Communication style preferences.
- Planning preferences such as preferred times, levels of detail, or decision-making style.
- Family-wide defaults, such as preferred airlines, hotels, restaurants, or activity types.

## What To Read From Here

Read this folder before making recommendations, buying suggestions, drafting messages, planning meals, or scheduling activities.

## Boundaries

Keep factual preferences separate from guesses. If a preference is inferred from behavior, label it as inferred and include the source or date.
