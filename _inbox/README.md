# Inbox

Use this folder for unprocessed notes, imports, transcripts, copied messages, or rough context that has not yet been sorted into the rest of the knowledge base.

## What To Put Here

- Raw notes from conversations with family members.
- Imported snippets that need review.
- Temporary observations that may become profile, routine, preference, or event updates.
- Questions that need a human answer before being filed elsewhere.

## What To Read From Here

Agents should read this folder early to find fresh context or unresolved updates. Treat anything here as provisional until it has been confirmed and moved into the appropriate durable folder.

## Maintenance

When processing an inbox note, either move the confirmed information into the right folder or leave a short status note explaining why it remains unresolved. Prefer dated filenames such as `2026-07-06-school-notes.md`.
