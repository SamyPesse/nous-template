# Household

Use this folder for practical information about the home and shared family operations.

## What To Put Here

- Addresses or address summaries where appropriate.
- Household members and room or space context.
- Pets, vehicles, recurring vendors, deliveries, and maintenance responsibilities.
- House rules, access instructions, and non-secret operational notes.

## What To Read From Here

Read this folder when helping with errands, deliveries, home maintenance, household planning, packing, or family logistics.

## What Not To Put Here

Do not store alarm codes, safe combinations, passwords, private keys, or full sensitive access credentials. Store those in a secure vault and reference their existence here only if useful.
