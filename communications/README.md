# Communications

Use this folder for reusable communication context, message preferences, and important communication history summaries.

## What To Put Here

- Preferred tone and language for messages to specific people or institutions.
- Draftable message templates for schools, caregivers, providers, or family members.
- Summaries of important communication threads when the details are useful later.
- Boundaries around what can be shared with whom.

## What To Read From Here

Read this folder before drafting emails, texts, Slack messages, school notes, or sensitive family communications.

## What Not To Put Here

Do not dump entire private message histories. Summarize the durable context and link to the source system when needed.
