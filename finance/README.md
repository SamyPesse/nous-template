# Finance

Use this folder for family finance context only when it helps with planning and administration.

## What To Put Here

- High-level budgets, allowance rules, reimbursement notes, subscription lists, and bill responsibility summaries.
- Renewal dates, payment cadence, and who owns a financial task.
- Links or references to secure financial systems.

## What To Read From Here

Read this folder before helping with budgeting, renewals, reimbursements, family purchases, subscriptions, or financial admin tasks.

## Privacy Boundary

Do not store bank credentials, full card numbers, account passwords, tax identifiers, or private financial statements here unless explicitly authorized. Prefer references and summaries.
