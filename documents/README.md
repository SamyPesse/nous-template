# Documents

Use this folder for references to important documents and lightweight summaries of where authoritative records live.

## What To Put Here

- Lists of important documents and where they are stored.
- Renewal dates, expiration dates, and responsible people.
- Form-filling notes that are safe to store in plaintext.
- Links to secure storage locations, Drive folders, or local paths when appropriate.

## What To Read From Here

Read this folder before helping with paperwork, travel, school forms, healthcare administration, renewals, or legal/financial logistics.

## Privacy Boundary

Avoid storing full passports, IDs, birth certificates, tax files, legal records, or scans here. This folder should usually contain metadata and pointers, not the sensitive originals.
