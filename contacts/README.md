# Contacts

Use this folder for important people, organizations, and service providers connected to family life.

## What To Put Here

- Doctors, schools, childcare providers, emergency contacts, neighbors, vendors, and recurring services.
- Preferred contact methods and availability windows.
- Notes about who to contact for specific situations.
- Links back to related profiles, routines, health, education, household, or document files.

## What To Read From Here

Read this folder before drafting outreach, making plans that involve third parties, preparing emergency context, or finding the right point of contact.

## Maintenance

Keep contact information current and date-stamped. Remove or mark stale contacts rather than leaving ambiguous entries.
