# Routines

Use this folder for recurring schedules, habits, school or work rhythms, childcare patterns, and household workflows.

## What To Put Here

- Morning, evening, school-day, weekend, travel, and bedtime routines.
- Recurring pickup/dropoff expectations.
- Meal planning rhythms, chore schedules, and recurring family commitments.
- Exceptions and seasonal variations.

## What To Read From Here

Read this folder before proposing plans, reminders, schedules, or automations. Routines often explain why a technically available time is not actually convenient.

## Maintenance

Include `Last updated` and note whether a routine is current, seasonal, or historical.
