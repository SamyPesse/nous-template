# Relationships

Use this folder for family structure, household relationships, caregiving relationships, and other important connections between people.

## What To Put Here

- Family trees or relationship maps.
- Notes about custody, caregiving, guardianship, or emergency responsibility.
- Social context that helps the assistant understand who should be included in plans or communications.
- Boundaries around who should or should not receive certain information.

## What To Read From Here

Read this folder before making assumptions about family roles, who can approve something, who should be invited, or how people are connected.

## Privacy Notes

Be precise and conservative. Do not infer sensitive relationship details unless they are explicitly provided or confirmed.
