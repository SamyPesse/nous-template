# Work

Use this folder for work, career, professional obligations, and availability context for family members.

## What To Put Here

- Work schedules and availability constraints.
- Employer or team context that affects family logistics.
- Travel obligations, recurring meetings, on-call rotations, or focus-time rules.
- Career goals or professional preferences if relevant to assistant support.

## What To Read From Here

Read this folder before scheduling, planning travel, drafting work-related messages, or balancing family commitments with professional obligations.

## What Not To Put Here

Do not store confidential employer data, proprietary documents, credentials, or private workplace messages unless explicitly authorized.
