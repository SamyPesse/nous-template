# Education

Use this folder for school, learning, classes, tutoring, and child development context.

## What To Put Here

- School names, class context, teacher or administrator contact references.
- Calendars, term dates, homework routines, learning goals, and accommodations.
- Notes from parent-teacher conversations or school decisions.
- Links to related contacts and routines.

## What To Read From Here

Read this folder before helping with school logistics, tutoring, homework support, calendar planning, forms, or communication with teachers.

## Maintenance

Separate current school-year information from historical notes. Use dated files for meetings and decisions.
